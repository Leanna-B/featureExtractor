Machine Training

Using the webcam, change the background color of your browser by training your computer to recognize when your left hand or right hand is raised. Take a minimum of 15 pictures of each hand raised, while clicking the blue button for the left hand and the red button for the right hand. When this is captured, click the "train button" and wait for the data to be processed. When this is done, click the "predict button" and the computer will now predict what hand is raised and change the web browser background color accordingly.
